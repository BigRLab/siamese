Similar Advertisement Search

SIAMESE allows users to search for similar images in three ways:

  • Paste a Delpher URL for an advertisement from Algemeen Handelsblad (1945-1969) or NRC Handelsblad (1970-1995) into the search box
  • Click on one of the images to use it as a source image
  • Click on ‘random’ for a random source image
Click on year to navigate to Delpher.
